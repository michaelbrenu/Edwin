Limitations & Future Work:
CLIP is a zero-shot model not fine-tuned on Ghanaian waste streams. A fine-tuned EfficientNet trained on local field photos would likely improve accuracy by 10–15 percentage points. Haar-cascade face detection achieves ~85% recall — production deployment should upgrade to YOLOv8-face for >98% recall. Ground-truth labelling of 500+ local images is recommended before municipal deployment.

Human-in-the-Loop & Ethical AI
  • 👤 All high-priority dispatch decisions are reviewed by an AMA supervisor before truck deployment.
  • ⚠ Low-confidence bins (<40%) are flagged for manual re-inspection before classification is accepted.
  • 🌍 CLIP was trained on web-scale data that under-represents Ghanaian waste streams — zero-shot accuracy may be lower for local informal-sector waste types.
  • 📋 A local fine-tuning dataset of ≥500 labelled Accra field photos is recommended before full municipal deployment.
  • 🔒 No personal data is stored or transmitted. Images are anonymised on-device before pipeline ingestion.
